MBTCs STO

"A New Paradigm
in Real Estate"

Easily diversified real estate investment

In the past, traditional real estate transactions have carried significant risk due to trading entire property ownership. However, MBTCs STO issues real estate ownership in the form of securities, allowing for decentralized investment. Investors receive dividends proportional to their investment, thereby increasing accessibility to real estate investment.

MBTCs STO Feature

Accessibility

Making real estate investment more accessible by allowing easy participation in what was once a difficult-to-approach market.

Liquidity

Real estate ownership can be easily bought and sold on a digital marketplace, providing liquidity to real estate capital akin to stocks.

Transparency

Based on blockchain technology, all investment records and real estate operational situations are transparently recorded and disclosed, showcasing the assets I've invested in transparently.
